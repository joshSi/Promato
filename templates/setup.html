The Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique distributes the total studying time into 25 minute intervals, separated by 5 minute short break. Each of these 25 + 5 minutes cycle is called a Pomodoro - tomatoes in Italian! Collect special tomotoes by studying!
